What is pair-programming?

Dev Bootcamp Wiki Pair Frank Shaw Stephens Xu

Pair-programming is the process where two programmers collectively work on programming assignment. During the process, one programmer would act as the Navigator, while the other act as the Driver.

Pair programming is important because it allows programmers to check over each other's work. It also helps with creative process because allows free flow of ideas between two individules. The navigator is able to catch catastrophic errors. Helps facilitate the coding process.
